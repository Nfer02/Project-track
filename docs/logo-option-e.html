Design Notes

Concept: The letter "P" is constructed with a bold geometric stem and an open bowl stroke. Inside the counter space (the enclosed area of the P), three ascending bars form a mini bar chart — embedding the "tracking/growth" concept directly into the letterform itself.


Why it works: At large sizes the bars are clearly visible, telling the brand story. At small sizes (16-32px) the bars merge into the overall letter shape and the P remains perfectly legible — it degrades gracefully without losing its identity.


Single color: The mark uses only #3b82f6. The bars are knocked out of the white bowl, revealing the blue background beneath. This means the logo works in pure monochrome — one color on any background.


App icon: The 28px corner radius on the container matches iOS icon specs. The mark sits comfortably within the safe area with even optical padding on all sides.
