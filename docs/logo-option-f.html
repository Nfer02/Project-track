Design Notes

Circle app-icon shape — Radial gradient from #60a5fa (bright) to #1d4ed8 (deep), with a top-left highlight that simulates a light source for a 3D feel.

Layered depth — Outer glow ring, inner shine overlay, bottom shadow gradient, and decorative arcs create multiple visual layers without clutter.

Bold geometric PT — Custom letter paths with rounded bowl on the P and strong horizontal bar on the T. White with drop shadows for contrast and depth.

Fintech accent — The subtle blue bar under the T adds a data/chart reference while breaking symmetry for visual interest.

Scalability — Decorative elements are progressively simplified at smaller sizes. At 24px the icon reduces to just the gradient circle and white letters for maximum legibility.
