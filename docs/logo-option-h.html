Design Notes

Concept: An isometric block structure that reads as the letter "P" from the front. A tall vertical column forms the stem, while a cantilevered upper block extends right and returns, creating the bowl. The dark counter-space between bowl and stem gives the P its unmistakable silhouette.

Three-face depth: Each visible face uses a different shade — light blue (#3b82f6) on top faces, medium blue (#2563eb) on right faces, dark blue (#1e40af) on left faces — creating true isometric dimension without any 3D rendering.

Growth accent: A small green (#10b981) upward arrow on the bowl's right face represents financial growth and project tracking. Subtle but visible at all sizes.

Architectural feel: The stepped base and block construction evoke building blocks — a metaphor for project management. The isometric perspective gives it a modern fintech/SaaS aesthetic.

Versatility: Works on dark backgrounds with the radial glow, on white with clean contrast, and inside rounded app icon containers. Readable even at 32px favicon scale.
